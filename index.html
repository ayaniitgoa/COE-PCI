WELCOME

IIT Goa has established a center of excellence to carry out multidisciplinary research with primary goal to develop cutting edge technical solution for challenging industrial problems. The center focuses primarily on three main domains, namely Particulates, Colloids, and Interfaces. Our present faculty members involved with the center have significant expertise in these domains. We envisioned developing this center as a platform for strong academic and industrial research collaboration. Industrial consultancies, advanced instrumentation development, short term course development for industry are some of the center’s key activities along with academic research.
